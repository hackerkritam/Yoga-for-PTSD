## üßò‚Äç‚ôÄÔ∏è The Five Therapeutic Poses

### Level 1: Mountain Pose (Tadasana) - Foundation
**Therapeutic Benefit**: Grounding and stability
**Hold Time**: 2 seconds
**Instructions**: 
- Stand straight with feet together
- Keep arms relaxed at your sides
- Look straight ahead
- Feel grounded and stable

### Level 2: Tree Pose (Vrikshasana) - Balance
**Therapeutic Benefit**: Focus and concentration
**Hold Time**: 3 seconds
**Instructions**:
- Stand on one leg
- Place your other foot on your thigh
- Bring hands to prayer position
- Focus on your balance point

### Level 3: Warrior Pose (Virabhadrasana) - Strength
**Therapeutic Benefit**: Confidence and inner strength
**Hold Time**: 2.5 seconds
**Instructions**:
- Step one foot back
- Bend your front knee
- Extend arms overhead
- Feel your inner warrior strength

### Level 4: Child's Pose (Balasana) - Surrender
**Therapeutic Benefit**: Relaxation and letting go
**Hold Time**: 2 seconds
**Instructions**:
- Kneel on the floor
- Sit back on your heels
- Fold forward with arms extended
- Surrender to peace and relaxation

### Level 5: Lotus Pose (Padmasana) - Meditation
**Therapeutic Benefit**: Mindfulness and inner peace
**Hold Time**: 3 seconds
**Instructions**:
- Sit cross-legged on the floor
- Place hands on your knees
- Keep spine straight
- Close your eyes and meditate

## üéØ Tips for Success

### Getting Started
1. **Find a quiet space** where you won't be interrupted
2. **Ensure good lighting** - the system needs to see you clearly
3. **Stand 6-8 feet from the camera** for optimal detection
4. **Wear comfortable clothing** that allows movement

### During Practice
1. **Follow the reference pose** on the right side of the screen
2. **Watch the accuracy percentage** - aim for 90% or higher
3. **Hold the pose steady** for the required time
4. **Breathe deeply** and stay relaxed

### Troubleshooting
- **If pose isn't detected**: Move closer to the camera
- **If accuracy is low**: Check the reference pose and adjust
- **If system is slow**: Close other applications
- **If camera doesn't work**: Check camera permissions
